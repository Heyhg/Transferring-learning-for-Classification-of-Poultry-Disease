Common Poultry Diseases

Coccidiosis

A parasitic disease of the intestinal tract that can cause bloody diarrhea and weight loss in poultry.

Newcastle Disease

A highly contagious viral disease that affects respiratory, nervous, and digestive systems of birds.

Salmonella

A bacterial infection that causes digestive problems and can be transmitted to humans through poultry products.
